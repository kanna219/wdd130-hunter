Welcome to Global Restrooms

“Traveling to another country can be exciting, but sometimes everyday things—like using the restroom—can become confusing. Different countries use different types of toilets and follow different bathroom customs. Travelers may encounter squat toilets, bidets, or even high-tech smart toilets that they have never seen before. Global Restrooms is designed to help travelers understand these differences before they travel. The website explains common restroom styles around the world and provides helpful tips for using them. Visitors can also rate restrooms based on cleanliness, accessibility, and comfort.”

Bathroom type/guide

Visitors to the Global Restrooms website will also be able to understand the different restrooms that we have listed and the proper use of them. This will help to make sure you can enjoy the trip preventing any possible accidents.

Ratings / Reviews

Visitors to the Global Restrooms website will also be able to rate their restroom experiences. This feature helps travelers learn what to expect in different places around the world.

Users can rate restrooms based on:

  • Location
  • Cleanliness
  • Comfort
  • Accessibility
  • Available supplies
  • Overall experience

Visitors can also write short reviews describing their experiences and giving helpful advice for other travelers.
